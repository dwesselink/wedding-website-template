RECTORY FARM

Our evening reception will be held at Rectory Farm, a country house and hotel about 10 minutes' drive (40 minutes' walk) from the centre of Cambridge. Parking will be available to all wedding guests on the day.

We encourage you to stay with us at Rectory Farm on the night of 16-17 September. The price for a double room will be £80, which includes breakfast. Some family suites (two connected bedrooms, sleeping four) are available for £160. All 37 rooms will be held for our guests until six weeks before the wedding. The RSVP form includes the option to book a room - there is no need to contact Rectory Farm directly.

OTHER PLACES TO STAY

Many Cambridge colleges rent out rooms as B&Bs during the summer vacation, which can be a fun alternative to staying in a city-centre hotel. The University Rooms website has more details; prices range from £50 - 130 on average.

Hotels within walking distance of Trinity Hall include the Varsity Hotel and the Regent Hotel. Further afield, there is a Premier Inn in Girton and a Travelodge and several independent B&Bs around the Cambridge train station.
