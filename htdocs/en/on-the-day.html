TRAVEL & PARKING

Be aware that the centre of Cambridge is ‘traffic-calmed’; bollards are up around the town centre on weekends and you will not be able to drive or park a private car in the streets closest to Trinity Hall.

If you intend to arrive in Cambridge by car, you are welcome to park at Rectory Farm for the day. Otherwise, you will need to park in one of the two paid public car parks within 5-10 minutes’ walk of Trinity Hall:

  • Park Street Car Park (map)
  • Grand Arcade Car Park, Corn Exchange St. (map)

These car parks fill up quickly on a Saturday, so you’ll need to arrive well in advance.

Our recommendation for guests with their own cars is:

  • Park at Rectory Farm on the morning of the wedding (map),
  • Take a taxi to Trinity Hall (approximately 10 minutes away),
  • Join our shuttle bus from Trinity Hall back to Rectory Farm after the ceremony

Taxis will be able to drop you off within 2 minutes’ walk from Trinity Hall. Cambridge is well-served for taxis, e.g. Panther Cabs (01223 715715). If you’re arriving by train, there is a large taxi rank at the Cambridge station. From the station, taxis will take around 10-15 minutes to reach Trinity Hall.
