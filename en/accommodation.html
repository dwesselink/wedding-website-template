RECTORY FARM

Our evening reception will be held at Rectory Farm, a country house and hotel about 10 minutes' drive (40 minutes' walk) from the centre of Cambridge. Parking will be available to all wedding guests on the day.

All 37 rooms will be held for our guests until six weeks before the wedding. When you receive your invitation in May, the RSVP will include an option to book a room.

OTHER PLACES TO STAY

Many Cambridge colleges rent out rooms as B&Bs during the summer vacation, which can be a fun alternative to staying in a city-centre hotel. The University Rooms website has more details; prices range from £50 - 130 on average.

Hotels within walking distance of Trinity Hall include the Varsity Hotel and the Regent Hotel. Further afield, there is the Premier Inn in Girton and a Travelodge and several independent B&Bs around the Cambridge train station.
